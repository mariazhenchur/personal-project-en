Vocabulary

Episode 1

sullen - angry and unwilling to smile or be pleasant to people. to abide - to accept or obey an agreement or rule. a qualm - an uncomfortable feeling when you doubt if you are doing the right thing. a sight for sore eyes - a way of saying that you're very pleased to see something. to be set on something - to be determined to do something. to make of something - to have an impression or understanding about something. to be cooped up somewhere - to live or be kept in a place which is too small or doesn't allow much freedom.

Episode 2

you don't say - to to express surprise or lack of surprise in a humorous slightly unkind way. famished - extremely hungry. to loiter - to move slowly around or stand in a public face without a reason. perfunctory - done quickly without taking care or showing interest. to put your best foot forward - to try as hard as possible to do something difficult.
